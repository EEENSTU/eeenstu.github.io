Message From
Chairman

Welcome to the website of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) Department of Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU).


The department has started its academic activities in B.Sc. (Eng.) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering program from January 2017.

It is my great pleasure to state that the department has well-qualified faculty members with professional experience in international arena. The faculty members are actively engaged in research in a diverse fields of Engineering, Science and Technology. The department has well equipped laboratories in all major areas of EEE for making students practically sound. We review the course curricula to update with the latest trends in EEE technologies on a regular basis.

I warmly invite you to browse the website of EEE Department to learn more about our undergraduate program, faculty members and their research interests underway in the department.


Professor Dr. Mohd Abdur Rashid
Chairman, Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering
