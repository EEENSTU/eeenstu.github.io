Mission

• To provide outstanding facilities to the students for gaining quality education in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
• To keep the course curricula up-to-date with the latest trends in EEE technologies on a regular basis.
• To create facilities for conducting high quality research in the field of Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
• To spread the information and knowledge picked up from the researches to the stakeholders through scholarly publications.

Vision

To create technology leaders in EEE who will take the challenges to meet the technological needs of the country in EEE fields.
